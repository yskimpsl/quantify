Who we are

BYOP is developed and operated by Quantify Inc. ("we", "us", or "our"), registered at 123 Cheomdangwagi-ro, Buk-gu, Gwangju 61005, South Korea. This policy explains how BYOP handles information — and, more simply, why there is very little to explain.

Questions? Reach us any time at yunsukim@quantify.co.kr.

02

Our commitment to privacy

We designed BYOP to do its job without knowing anything about you. The app runs locally on your device and does not require an account, a sign-in, or an internet connection to protect your privacy. We have no servers that receive your information, because BYOP does not send us any.

03

Information we collect

None. BYOP does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or account credentials.
  • Usage analytics, behavioral data, or telemetry.
  • Crash or diagnostic reports sent to us.
  • Location, contacts, photos, or device identifiers.
  • Any other data that could identify you.

Because we collect nothing, there is no personal information for us to use, share, sell, or lose.

04

Data on your device

Any content, files, or settings you create in BYOP stay on your own device. We have no access to them, and they are never uploaded to us or to anyone else. You remain in full control: removing that data — for example by deleting it in the app or uninstalling BYOP — removes it from your device.

05

Third-party services and advertising

BYOP does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app displays no advertisements and includes no third-party SDKs that would collect your information. We do not share data with third parties, because we hold none.

06

Children's privacy

BYOP does not collect personal information from anyone, including children. The app contains no data collection, advertising, or tracking that would raise privacy concerns for younger users.

07

Security

The most effective protection for your data is that we never receive it. Since BYOP keeps everything on your device and transmits nothing to us, there is no personal information in our custody that could be exposed in a breach. Protecting the data on your device is handled by your device's own security and the operating system.

08

Your rights

Privacy laws such as the GDPR and Korea's PIPA give you rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Because we do not hold any personal information about you, there is nothing for us to retrieve or erase on our side. If you have any questions about your privacy, contact us at yunsukim@quantify.co.kr and we will be glad to help.

09

Changes to this policy

If a future version of BYOP changes how it handles information, we will update this policy and revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page before that version is released. Your continued use of the app after an update means you accept the revised policy.
